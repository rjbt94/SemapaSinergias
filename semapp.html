Mission and Values


Autonomy

This web platform does not intend to take away/ eliminate the current structures autonomy.

Communication & Share

One of the keypoints is to improve communication, knowledge & work practices across the group & with suppliers.

Simplified Work-flows

Semapp supply aggregates private & public work flows linked with independent softwares.

Friendly & Coordinated

This is not a project to increase work load for anyone. Must be user-friendly & lined up with our needs.

Dedicated & Customized Support/Training

This only works with adequate training and external technical support.

A "SOURCE TO PAY" TOOL FOR ALL SEMAPA PARTNERS

...with automated SUPPLYING PROCEDURES & SIMPLYFING PROCUREMENT activities, enchacing the relationship between Semapa Companies and our Suppliers!

Create Value

Semapa has ambition to move from an Active Investment Manager to a Value Creation Partner, which means higher support in operations & strategies and holding companies working side by side, creating value for the whole group .

Save Money

The goal is to work together in selected/common supplying and purchasing areas (taking advantage of the experience & know-how of the different companies and stakeholders). The ambition is to save money and have more control of suppliers quality matched with our criteria and principles

Agile Process

Share2Improve

We can share everyhing useful for Procurement and Purchasing Departments of the different companies: Tendering processes, Enquiries, Procedures, Contracts, Prices, Suppliers database, evaluation systems, codes, market forecasts, rankings, lessons learned, new projects, etc. The suppliers can develop a strong relationship with the whole group and race with competitors to increase their efficiency, portfolio, quality and price (matched with our needs).
